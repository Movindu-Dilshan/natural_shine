Instructions

How to Use Our Herbal Oil

For the best results, follow these simple steps to deeply nourish your hair.

  1. Step 1: Gently warm a small amount of oil. Take the required amount in a bowl and place it in warm water for a few minutes.
  2. Step 2: Part your hair and apply the oil directly to the scalp using your fingertips.
  3. Step 3: Massage the scalp in a circular motion for 5-10 minutes to improve blood circulation.
  4. Step 4: Work the oil through the length of your hair to the tips. Leave it on for at least one hour, or overnight for a deep conditioning treatment.
  5. Step 5: Wash off with a mild, herbal shampoo. Use 2-3 times a week for visible results.
